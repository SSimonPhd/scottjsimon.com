intro

Scott J. Simon, PhD (University of North Texas) is a classical guitarist and composer based in Austin, Texas. His work explores non-functional harmony, altered chords, and meditative textures rooted in classical guitar, jazz improvisation, and contemporary styles.


Performances span intimate settings to prestigious stages, blending sophistication, warmth, and nuanced tone.


"The technique of the guitar should be like the air, only felt when it is not there." – Andrés Segovia


He currently offers limited private lessons focused on sight-reading, technique, theory, vocabulary, and repertoire, tailored to each student. The goal is always the same: to make the difficult feel effortless and let the music speak for itself.


For lesson inquiries, performance bookings, or collaborations, contact simon_phd@proton.me
